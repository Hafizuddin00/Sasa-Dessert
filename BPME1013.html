BPME1013 - Introduction to Entrepreneurship

Business Progress

From initial concept to successful execution, SaSa Dessert represents our complete entrepreneurial journey in BPME1013. Starting with market research and business planning, we developed a student-focused cake business that serves the UUM community. Through challenges in operations, marketing, and customer service, we've grown from a simple idea to a thriving enterprise with 70+ satisfied customers. This documentation captures our learning process, strategic decisions, financial management, and the valuable lessons learned as we conclude this entrepreneurial venture.
